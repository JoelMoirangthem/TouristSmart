About Varanasi

The spiritual heart of India

Ancient Heritage

One of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities, dating back over 3,000 years.

Sacred Ganges

The holy river Ganges flows through the city, making it a sacred pilgrimage destination.

Spiritual Center

Home to over 2,000 temples and countless spiritual traditions and practices.

Must-Visit Attractions

Discover the wonders of Varanasi

Dashashwamedh Ghat

The most famous ghat where the spectacular Ganga Aarti ceremony takes place every evening.

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

One of the most famous Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in the heart of Varanasi.

Banaras Hindu University

One of the largest residential universities in Asia, known for its academic excellence and campus beauty.

Sunrise at Ganges

Experience the magical sunrise boat ride on the holy Ganges river for a spiritual awakening.

Ramnagar Fort

A historic fort and palace complex showcasing the rich cultural heritage of Varanasi.

Sarnath

The deer park where Buddha first taught the Dharma, a must-visit Buddhist pilgrimage site.
